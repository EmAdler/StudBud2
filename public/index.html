STUDbud

Studbud is the smart web application that assist all uni students to pass their degree! It I an interactive program that has a range of tools to reduce procrastination and maximise study efficiency

How to get the most out of this application

  • 1. Add all your tasks to the task list
  • 2. Add all details including, time due, the subject, the weight etc.
  • 3. Block your study time out by using the timer, to reduce burn out
  • 4. Play music to reduce your distractions
